About Rohanpreet

Rohanpreet Singh is a versatile singer, performer, songwriter, actor and television personality. He grew up in a musically nurturing environment encouraged by his father's Uncle, trained in Hindustani Classical Music, he began singing at the tender age of three — a passion that soon grew into a promising career.

At the age of 11, he gained nationwide recognition in 2007 through Sa Re Ga Ma Pa L'il Champs, which opened the doors for him to perform in live shows and concerts across India and abroad.

A decade later, in 2018, he once again captured hearts as the first runner-up of the popular reality show Rising Star. A young, determined artist with a natural ability to connect with audiences.

With his dedication, versatility, and ever-growing fan base, Rohanpreet Singh stands as an inspiring figure in the world of Indian music, steadily carving his place as both a beloved performer and a relatable, modern artist.
